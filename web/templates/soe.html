How we started...

While our vision was always focused on enabling innovation through education in Africa's tertiary institutions, we started out as a learning platform designed for students in tertiarly institutions to combat failure by puting all learning resources in one place to help students learn at their own schedule, at their own pace and pass their courses with good grades.

...where we are.

In a mission pivot, we are now focused on achieving our vision by enabling peer learning and connecting students together to achieve more.
